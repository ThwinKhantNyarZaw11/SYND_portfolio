Hospital Structural Analysis

Project Overview

Conducted advanced structural analysis and seismic design for a new multi-wing hospital complex. The project prioritized resilience, emergency functionality, and structural redundancy to ensure safety and long-term performance in a critical healthcare facility.

Key Responsibilities

  • Performed load analysis and lateral force resistance design using advanced modeling
  • Engineered expansion joints and isolation systems for seismic activity
  • Integrated steel and concrete hybrid systems for long-span operating theaters
  • Designed structural fireproofing and equipment support frameworks (e.g. MRI, HVAC)
  • Collaborated with architects for floor vibration control and emergency egress routing
