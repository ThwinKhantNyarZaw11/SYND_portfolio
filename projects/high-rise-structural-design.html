High-Rise Structural Design

Project Overview

As lead architectural engineer, I was responsible for designing the structural framework for a 30-story mixed-use tower in a seismic-prone region. The project emphasized stability, sustainability, and construction efficiency while meeting international safety codes.

  • Performed seismic load calculations and finite element modeling (FEM)
  • Collaborated with MEP teams for vertical shaft coordination

  • Optimized column placements to maximize open floor space
